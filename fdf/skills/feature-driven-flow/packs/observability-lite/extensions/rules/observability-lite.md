# Rule: observability-lite

## id
`observability-lite`

## title
Observability Lite (Workflow Debug Notes)

## tags
`observability`, `metrics`, `team`

## applies_to_phases
`scope`, `verify`, `summarize`

## intent
Capture a small, consistent set of workflow debugging notes (what changed, why, and where friction happened) to improve future runs.

## guidance
### scope
1. Record a short "baseline plan" note in the run artifacts:
   - expected phases to be most risky
   - expected constraints and likely blockers

### verify
1. Record "verification delta" notes:
   - what was harder than expected to verify
   - any missing evidence you wish you had earlier

### summarize
1. Record "retro lite" notes:
   - 1-2 things that worked well
   - 1-2 friction points
   - 1 actionable improvement for settings/rules/templates

## checks
### scope
1. Baseline workflow debug notes are recorded.

### verify
1. Verification delta notes are recorded.

### summarize
1. Retro lite notes are recorded.

## outputs
### scope
1. Baseline workflow debug notes.

### verify
1. Verification delta notes.

### summarize
1. Retro lite notes.
