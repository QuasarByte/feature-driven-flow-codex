# Rule: clarify-policy

## id
`clarify-policy`

## title
Clarify Policy

## tags
`process`, `clarify`

## applies_to_phases
`clarify`

## intent
Resolve ambiguity before architecture and implementation decisions.

## guidance
### clarify
1. Ask decision-ready questions for edge behavior and failure semantics.
2. Clarify compatibility commitments and data/interface contracts.
3. Clarify performance and security expectations.
4. Stay blocked when decision-critical ambiguity remains.

## checks
### clarify
1. Required decisions are explicit.
2. Blocking ambiguity is either resolved or clearly marked.

## outputs
### clarify
1. Prioritized clarification question list.
2. Assumptions and decision log.
